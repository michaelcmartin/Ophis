II. To HLL and Back

This is a compilation of an essay series I wrote from 2002-2005 explaining how to apply HLL constructs from high-level languages in your assembly language projects.

The examples have been updated and modernized for Ophis 2, and while the examples all target the Commodore 64, they are more generally applicable.
